SME Projection (Fiji/ImageJ)
Maintainer
Source on GitHub
Category Plugins, Analysis

Smooth manifold extraction (SME) is a remarkable alternative to the traditional Maximum Intensity Projection (MIP). You can read more about it in the SME manuscript. Code and binaries are available on GitHub.

Publication

  • Shihavuddin, Asm; Basu, Sreetama & Rexhepaj, Elton et al. (2017 May 31), "Smooth 2D manifold extraction from 3D image stack.", Nature Communications 8, PMID 28561033, doi:10.1038/ncomms15554, <https://www.nature.com/articles/ncomms15554>.
