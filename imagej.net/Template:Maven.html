Usage

{{Maven
| g     =
| a     =
| v     =
| p     =
| c     =
| label =
}}

Examples

Link to all versions of a particular artifact by specifying g (i.e., groupId) and a (i.e., artifactId): 

{{Maven | g=net.imagej | a=imagej}}

net.imagej:imagej


Link to a specific version by also specifying v (i.e., version): 

{{Maven | g=net.imagej | a=imagej | v=2.0.0-rc-30}}

net.imagej:imagej:2.0.0-rc-30


You can also specify p for packaging, and c for classifier, if you need those options: 

{{Maven | g=net.imagej | a=imagej-launcher | p=nar | c=amd64-Linux-gcc-executable}}

net.imagej:imagej-launcher:nar:amd64-Linux-gcc-executable
