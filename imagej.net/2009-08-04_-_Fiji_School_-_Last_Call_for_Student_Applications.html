2009-08-04 - Fiji School - Last Call for Student Applications

Fiji School

* * Last call for student applications * *

The International Neuroinformatics Coordinating Facility is hosting an Image Processing School based on Fiji, as a satellite event to the Neuroinformatics 2009 Congress.

The Image Processing School will instruct students on digital image processing, focusing on image registration and segmentation, 3D visualization, image analysis and programming. See the school webpage for all details.

The instructors are all Fiji developers.

Student applications are now open.

Student travel fellowships available, limited to students enrolled in a Master or PhD program in Europe.


Important data:

  • Where: Pilsen, Czech Republic
  • When: September 9-12, 2009
  • School webpage
  • Student application form.
