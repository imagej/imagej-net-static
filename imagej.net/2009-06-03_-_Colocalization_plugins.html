2009-06-03 - Colocalization plugins

By request of Daniel James White, we now have Colocalization plugins in Fiji. We took two plugins from the MBF Plugin Collection, fixed them to work with newer ImageJ versions, and put them into the Fiji Updater. Oh, and of course, they are on GitHub, too.
