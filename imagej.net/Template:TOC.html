Template:TOC

Template for including the table of contents.

Usage

Include a TOC at the top, not wrapped: 

{{TOC}}


Include a TOC which floats left: 

{{TOC|left}}


Include a TOC which floats right: 

{{TOC|right}}


Include a TOC with small font: 

{{TOC|small}}
