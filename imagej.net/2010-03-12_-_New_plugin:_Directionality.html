2010-03-12 - New plugin: Directionality

Jean-Yves Tinevez released the Directionality plugin, which allows you to quantify the orientation of the structures in your images. It does so by analyzing either the Fourier spectrum, or the local image gradient. Check it out, it is pretty cool!
